Carbonara Pasta

Description

A classic Italian Carbonara, made with creamy eggs, crispy pancetta, and Parmesan cheese, creating a rich and silky sauce that perfectly coats the pasta. Simple ingredients, bold flavors!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti (12 oz)
  • Pancetta or bacon (4 oz, diced)
  • Eggs (2 large + 1 egg yolk)
  • Parmesan cheese (1 cup, grated)
  • Black pepper (to taste)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Garlic (1 clove, optional, minced)
  • Olive oil (1 tbsp, if needed)

Preparation

  1. Cook the pasta – Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Reserve about 1 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. Prepare the sauce – In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, egg yolk, grated Parmesan cheese, and a generous amount of black pepper.
  3. Cook the pancetta – In a large pan over medium heat, cook the diced pancetta (or bacon) until crispy. Add minced garlic if using, then remove from heat.
  4. Combine pasta and pancetta – Add the drained pasta to the pan with the pancetta, tossing to coat in the rendered fat.
  5. Add the sauce – Remove the pan from heat and quickly stir in the egg and cheese mixture, tossing constantly to create a creamy sauce. Add reserved pasta water a little at a time if needed for a silky texture.
  6. Adjust seasoning – Taste and adjust with more black pepper and Parmesan if desired.
  7. Serve immediately – Plate the Carbonara and garnish with extra cheese and black pepper. Enjoy!
