Carbonara Pasta

Description

A classic Italian Carbonara, made with creamy eggs, crispy pancetta, and Parmesan cheese, creating a rich and silky sauce that perfectly coats the pasta. Simple ingredients, bold flavors!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti (12 oz)
  • Pancetta or bacon (4 oz, diced)
  • Eggs (2 large + 1 egg yolk)
  • Parmesan cheese (1 cup, grated)
  • Black pepper (to taste)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Garlic (1 clove, optional, minced)
  • Olive oil (1 tbsp, if needed)
