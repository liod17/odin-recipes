Ice Cream

Description

A rich and creamy animal-based mango ice cream made with fresh mangoes, heavy cream, egg yolks, and milk. This indulgent treat is packed with natural sweetness and a velvety texture for the perfect tropical dessert!

Ingredients

  • Ripe mangoes (2, peeled and diced)
  • Heavy cream (1 cup)
  • Whole milk (1 cup)
  • Egg yolks (4)
  • Honey (½ cup, adjust to taste)
  • Vanilla extract (1 tsp)
  • Lemon juice (1 tbsp, optional, to enhance flavor)

Preparation

  1. Blend the mangoes – Puree the diced mangoes in a blender until smooth. Set aside.
  2. Heat the milk and cream – In a saucepan over medium heat, combine the whole milk and heavy cream. Heat until warm but not boiling.
  3. Whisk the egg yolks and honey – In a separate bowl, whisk the egg yolks and honey until smooth and slightly pale.
  4. Temper the eggs – Slowly pour a small amount of the warm milk mixture into the egg yolks while whisking continuously to prevent curdling.
  5. Cook the custard – Pour the tempered egg mixture back into the saucepan. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens slightly and coats the back of a spoon. Do not let it boil.
  6. Strain and cool – Strain the custard through a fine-mesh sieve to remove any lumps, then let it cool to room temperature.
  7. Mix in mango and vanilla – Stir the mango puree and vanilla extract into the cooled custard, mixing well.
  8. Chill the mixture – Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight for better flavor.
  9. Churn the ice cream – Pour the chilled mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  10. Freeze until firm – Transfer the churned ice cream to a container and freeze for at least 2 hours before serving.
