Ground Beef

Description

A hearty and flavorful ground beef recipe that’s perfect for a quick meal. Seasoned beef is sautéed with onions, garlic, and spices, then simmered in a rich sauce. Serve it over rice, pasta, or as a filling for tacos and wraps!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef (1 lb)
  • Onion (1, diced)
  • Garlic (2 cloves, minced)
  • Olive oil (1 tbsp)
  • Tomato sauce (1 cup)
  • Worcestershire sauce (1 tbsp)
  • Paprika (1 tsp)
  • Cumin (1/2 tsp)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Black pepper (to taste)
  • Red pepper flakes (optional, for heat)
  • Fresh parsley (for garnish)
