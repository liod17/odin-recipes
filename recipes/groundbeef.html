Ground Beef

Description

A hearty and flavorful ground beef recipe that’s perfect for a quick meal. Seasoned beef is sautéed with onions, garlic, and spices, then simmered in a rich sauce. Serve it over rice, pasta, or as a filling for tacos and wraps!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef (1 lb)
  • Onion (1, diced)
  • Garlic (2 cloves, minced)
  • Olive oil (1 tbsp)
  • Tomato sauce (1 cup)
  • Worcestershire sauce (1 tbsp)
  • Paprika (1 tsp)
  • Cumin (1/2 tsp)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Black pepper (to taste)
  • Red pepper flakes (optional, for heat)
  • Fresh parsley (for garnish)

Preparation

  1. Heat the oil – In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Sauté the onions and garlic – Add the diced onion and minced garlic, cooking until softened and fragrant.
  3. Cook the ground beef – Add the ground beef to the pan, breaking it apart with a spatula. Cook until browned and no longer pink.
  4. Drain excess fat – If necessary, drain any excess grease from the pan.
  5. Season the beef – Stir in salt, black pepper, paprika, cumin, and red pepper flakes (if using).
  6. Add the sauces – Pour in the tomato sauce and Worcestershire sauce, stirring well to combine.
  7. Simmer – Reduce the heat and let the mixture simmer for about 5–10 minutes, allowing the flavors to blend.
  8. Garnish and serve – Sprinkle fresh parsley on top and serve over rice, pasta, or in tacos!
